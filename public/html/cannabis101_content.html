What is Cannabis?

Cannabis is a plant that has been cultivated and used for thousands of years. It serves as both a recreational substance and a source of medical benefits.

Basics of Cannabis

  • Strains: Sativa, Indica, Hybrid
  • Components: THC, CBD, Terpenes
  • Forms: Flower, Edibles, Concentrates, Topicals
  • Consumption Methods: Smoking, Vaping, Eating, Applying
